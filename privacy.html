Learious - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. About Learious

Learious is a social media application operated by Lumo that creates and distributes physics explainer animations on platforms including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. This Privacy Policy explains how Learious collects, uses, and protects information.

2. Information We Collect

Learious collects minimal information necessary to provide the service. When you interact with content published through Learious on social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X), the respective platform privacy policies apply. Learious may collect publicly available engagement data (likes, comments, views) for analytics purposes to improve content quality.

3. How Learious Uses Information

Learious uses collected information solely to improve content quality and understand audience preferences. We do not sell, trade, or transfer personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Learious integrates with third-party platforms including YouTube (Google), TikTok (ByteDance), Instagram (Meta), and X (X Corp). Each platform has its own privacy policy governing data collected through their services. Learious uses their APIs solely for content publishing and analytics purposes.

5. TikTok API Usage

Learious uses the TikTok API to publish video content. When using the TikTok integration, Learious accesses only the permissions necessary for content publishing. Learious does not store TikTok user data beyond what is required for the publishing workflow.

6. Data Security

Learious implements reasonable security measures to protect any information handled. API credentials and access tokens are stored securely and never shared publicly.

7. Data Retention

Learious retains analytics data only as long as necessary for content improvement purposes. Users may request deletion of any personal data by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries about Learious, please contact us through our social media channels or visit our website.
